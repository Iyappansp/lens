Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our eye care services, eyewear, and ordering process.

Eye Tests & Appointments

How do I book an eye test?

Visit our Eye Test Booking page, select a convenient date and time, fill in your details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation by email shortly after.

How long does an eye exam take?

A comprehensive eye exam typically takes 30–45 minutes, depending on whether additional tests are needed.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes — contact our team at least 24 hours in advance via the Contact page to reschedule or cancel.

Frames & Virtual Try-On

Can I try frames virtually before buying?

Yes! Our Virtual Try-On tool lets you preview frame styles instantly using your photo or live camera preview before deciding.

How do I know which frame shape suits me?

Visit our Face Shape Guide for a breakdown of recommended frame shapes by face type, or book a free styling consultation.

Do you offer adjustments after purchase?

Yes, complimentary frame adjustments are included with every purchase for the lifetime of the frame.

Lenses & Prescriptions

How long does lens customization take?

Most prescription lenses are ready within 48–72 hours. Specialized coatings like photochromic may take slightly longer.

What's the difference between single vision and progressive lenses?

Single vision corrects one field of distance, while progressives blend near, intermediate, and far vision into one seamless lens. See our Lens Solutions page for details.

Insurance & Payments

Do you accept vision insurance?

We work with most major vision insurance providers. Visit our Insurance Benefits page for accepted plans and the claims process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, insurance direct billing, and select buy-now-pay-later providers.
